The endless cycle ends here

Todoist. Asana. Notion. TickTick. You've tried them all. And somehow, you always end up back at Google Calendar, stitching tasks together with workarounds.

Detask doesn't replace your calendar — it completes it. It feels like a native part of Google Calendar, because that's exactly how it was designed.

Built for how you actually work

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Unified inbox + Today view

All incoming tasks land in one inbox. Switch to Today to manage your day and plan for tomorrow, right alongside your meetings.

Keyboard-first

Shortcuts for everything. Triage, schedule, move, complete — never reach for the mouse.

AI chat

Ask anything about your tasks and lists. Get stats, find what you need, and organize intuitively with natural language.

Rich context in lists

Add Notion-like rich content to your tasks. Notes, checklists, links — everything lives with the task, not in a separate doc.

Recurring tasks + notifications

Set up repeating tasks and reminders that feel just like Google Calendar events. Same logic, same reliability.

Overdue? No guilt

Quickly reschedule, snooze, or batch-move overdue tasks. Beat procrastination instead of drowning in red labels.
